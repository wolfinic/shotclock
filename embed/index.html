How to embed the shot clock

  • Choose a clock — 24s (NBA/FIBA), 30s (NCAA), 35s (high school) or 12s (3x3).
  • Pick a size, or choose Responsive to let the clock fill its container.
  • Copy the generated snippet and paste it into your page's HTML where you want the clock.

The snippet is a single <iframe> that loads the live ProShotClock app in compact embed mode (just the clock and its controls — no menus or page chrome). Because it points to the live app, every improvement shows up automatically with zero maintenance on your side.
